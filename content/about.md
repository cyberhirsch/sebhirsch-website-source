# Seb Hirsch

I am a multifaceted professional with extensive experience in academia, visual effects (VFX), motion design, generative AI, and 3D animation. 

Currently, I serve as a **Professor for Time-Based Media and 3D Animation** at Media University (formerly HMKW). My academic work focus is on the intersection of technology and creativity, preparing the next generation of digital artists.

## Industry Background

My professional journey includes work on major international productions. In 2012, I was part of the team recognized with a **Primetime Emmy Award** for outstanding visual effects on *Game of Thrones* (Season 2). 

Beyond high-end television, I have contributed to significant feature films, such as creating the digital environments for *Berlin 36*, and have worked across various software pipelines including 3ds Max, Blender, and Unreal Engine.

## Specialized Expertise

*   **Visual Effects:** High-end compositing, environment creation, and technical direction.
*   **Photogrammetry:** 3D scanning and cultural heritage preservation.
*   **Generative AI:** Research and practical application of AI in creative workflows.
*   **Artistic Practice:** Experimenting with analog and digital photography, often blending traditional techniques with modern AI and 3D tools.

Based in Cologne, Germany, I continue to consult for industry pipelines while leading academic research in time-based media.
