Contributed as a **Matte Painter** at **Pixomondo** for the 2013 blockbuster *Star Trek Into Darkness*. 

The work involved creating large-scale futuristic environments and set extensions, blending digital painting with 3D elements to define the visual scape of the J.J. Abrams sequel.
