Sebastian worked as a VFX/Environment Artist at **Pixomondo** for the acclaimed HBO series *Game of Thrones*. 

He was part of the talented team recognized with a **Primetime Emmy Award** in 2012 for *Outstanding Special Visual Effects* for Season 2. His work involved creating complex digital environments and seamless integration to bring the world of Westeros to life.
