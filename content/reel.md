### About the Reel
This showreel represents some of the visual effects work I've been fortunate to contribute to over the years. It includes shots from various projects where I played different roles, including some work on **Star Trek Into Darkness** and **Game of Thrones**. 
 
You'll see full CG animations from films like **2012** and **U900**, where I worked on elements such as shading, lighting, and FX. I also had the chance to create set extensions and matte paintings for movies including **The Ghostwriter** and **Hotel Lux**.
