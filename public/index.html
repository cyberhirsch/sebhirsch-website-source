Visual Effects & 3D Animation

This showreel represents some of the visual effects work I've been fortunate to contribute to over the years. It includes shots from various projects where I played different roles, including work on 'Star Trek Into Darkness' and 'Game of Thrones'.

I've had the chance to create set extensions and matte paintings for movies including 'The Ghostwriter' and 'Hotel Lux'. The reel also shows the wide range of my work from title animations to crowd simulations.

While many of these shots were collaborative efforts with talented teams, I hope this gives a sense of the areas I've been able to work in and develop skills over time.
